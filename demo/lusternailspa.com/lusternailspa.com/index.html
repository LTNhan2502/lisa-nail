About Us

Discover a Premier
Nails & Spa Experience

Welcome to LUSTER | nails salon & spa — where beauty and relaxation meet. We offer premium nail care and spa services delivered by a dedicated team that values cleanliness, attention to detail, and your personal style.

At LUSTER, choose from a full menu: Nail Enhancement (Dip/Acrylic), Gel or Regular Manicures, relaxing Spa Pedicures, trendy Gel X Fullsets, kid-friendly services, and professional Waxing. Every service is designed with hygiene, safety, and comfort in mind.

We celebrate creativity — from classic to modern shapes, soft to statement colors, and finishes like Glitter or Color Powder. Everything is customized to your vibe. Bring the inspiration; our team will take care of the rest.

Visit LUSTER | nails salon & spa to enjoy a warm atmosphere, thoughtful service, and a flawless finish that exceeds expectations. Book your appointment today and feel the difference.

Our Services

Welcome to Smiling Nails & Lash. We offer professional services for manicure, pedicure, eyelash extensions, and waxing. Visit us and let our professional team pamper you.

Nail Enhancement

Professional dipping powder and acrylic services to enhance your natural nails with beautiful, long-lasting results.

Manicure

Let your nails shine with our professional manicure services, including gel and regular polish options.

Pedicures

Enjoy a relaxing pedicure experience with our range of spa treatments from classic to deluxe services.

Gel X Fullset

Get beautiful, durable nails with our Gel X system in any shape and length you desire.

Kid's Services

Special services designed for children 11 and younger, including manicures, pedicures, and fun designs.

Waxing

Professional waxing services for eyebrows, facial hair, and other areas to help you achieve smooth, hair-free skin.

Art Gallery

Discover our beautiful artistic works. From delicate nail art to unique designs, each image showcases our dedication and professional craftsmanship.
